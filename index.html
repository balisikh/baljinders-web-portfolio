About Me

Hello, I'm Baljinder Singh Rai, a dedicated and disciplined learner with a strong desire to contribute to the success of website development teams. I embarked on my journey by enrolling in the Level 3 Software Development Bootcamp at Just IT Online on July 3, 2023, where I had the privilege of learning from highly knowledgeable trainers. During this program, I acquired expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySql, and Python.

My main goals are in creating responsive websites that meet the needs of the clients ensuring that websites are functioning properly using QA and Software Testing, communicating with other stakeholders during website implementation, enhancing creativity in web designs and optimising the speed performance of webpages. I am a future Front End Developer with the passion of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I enjoy solving debugging problems of the VS code, and I like to take on new challenges.

My primary objectives revolve around crafting responsive websites tailored to clients' needs, ensuring flawless functionality through rigorous QA and software testing, effective communication with stakeholders during website implementation, fostering creativity in web design, and optimizing webpage performance for speed. I'm passionate about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I thrive on tackling debugging challenges within VS Code, always ready to embrace new challenges.

Recently, I achieved a significant milestone as Southall Community College (also known as West London College Southall) honored me with a final grade for my two-year journey in the BTEC Level 3 ICT Extended Diploma program. I proudly earned two Distinctions and one Merit, underscoring my commitment to excellence.

As I look ahead, my aspirations lead me towards a Level 4 Software Development Apprenticeship following the completion of the 12-week Online Software Development Bootcamp in Level 3. My ultimate goal is to gain valuable experience that will pave the way for a rewarding career in web development.
